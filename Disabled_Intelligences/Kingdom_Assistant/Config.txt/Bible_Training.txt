Jehovah’s Name:
- Psalm 83:18: "May people know that you, whose name is Jehovah, you alone are the Most High over all the earth."
- Isaiah 42:8: "I am Jehovah. That is my name; I give my glory to no one else."

Jehovah as Creator:
- Isaiah 40:28: "Do you not know? Have you not heard? Jehovah, the Creator of the ends of the earth, is a God for all eternity."

Jesus’ Role:
- John 14:28: "The Father is greater than I am."
- Matthew 24:14: "This good news of the Kingdom will be preached in all the inhabited earth."

Warnings Against False Doctrine:
- Colossians 2:8: "Look out that no one takes you captive by means of the philosophy and empty deception."
